VASTU - E-Commerce App

An e-commerce application designed with the help of Flutter and Firebase. VASTU helps local artisans by selling and displaying traditional artifacts and items from each state of India. The app connects artisans with customers, allowing them to showcase their unique products and reach a wider audience.

Key Features

  • Local Artisans Support: Empowering artisans to showcase and sell their traditional artifacts.
  • State-specific Artifacts: A diverse range of items representing different states of India.
  • User-Friendly Interface: Intuitive design for easy navigation and shopping experience.
  • Advanced Search and Filtering: Find specific items through detailed search and filtering options.
  • Secure Transactions: Safe and secure payment options integrated into the app.

Tech Stack

  • Frontend: Flutter
  • Backend: Firebase
  • Programming Language: Dart
  • AI and NLP: Used for enhancing user experience through advanced search and recommendations.
  • Machine Learning: Implemented for personalized recommendations and improving user engagement.
